02 Standardization & Reorganization

What was accomplished

  • 100% naming compliance — every family migrated to B_<CAT>_<Subtype>_<Descriptor> with a consistent 4-letter category code.
  • Folder structure rationalized into 16 standard categories; legacy folders folded into the standard set Accessories-BathroomEquipmentKitchens & MillworkFire ProtectionSecurity Devices
  • Whole pipeline realigned — Guardian Pass & Cleaned rebuilt to mirror the Audited structure, hash-verified with zero content change.
  • Duplicate families removed — redundant / mis-coded copies consolidated across all three stages.

Nested-family documentation

  • 1,248 nested-family relationships catalogued across the cleaned library — every dependency mapped one level at a time (A→B, B→C).
  • Kitchen (36) & Bathroom (37) unit families fully audited — parent/child links, type variants, sizes and status — into dedicated Notion databases + Excel.
  • Gives a queryable, always-current picture of what each assembly is built from.
